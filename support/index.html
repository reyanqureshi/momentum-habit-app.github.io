Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new habit?

Tap the + button on the home screen and select "New Habit". Give it a name, pick an emoji and color, then set your preferred time of day and category.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud enabled, it syncs across your devices through Apple's CloudKit. We never have access to your data.

How do I restore my Premium purchase?

Go to Settings, tap "Momentum Premium", then tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the paywall screen. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to your device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions, find Momentum, and tap Cancel Subscription.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. You can delete individual habits from within the app, or use the "Reset All Data" option in Settings. Deleting the app also removes all local data.

Notifications aren't working. What should I do?

Make sure notifications are enabled for Momentum in your device's Settings > Notifications > Momentum. Also check that Do Not Disturb or a Focus mode isn't blocking them.
